To manage your weight you have to exercise, and eat healthy diet. It is advised to have a personalized plan that fits your life style and food preferences. This plan may involve making major changes, some tougher than others.

Don't try to change everything at once. Try to focus on achievable goals, such as walking to the corner store, if you usually take your car. Although these small changes may not seem like much in the beginning, after a while, they can add up to become part of your daily routine with measurable health benefits.
